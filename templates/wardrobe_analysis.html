ℹ️ Understanding These Metrics
Cost Per Wear (CPW)
CPW = Purchase Price ÷ Times Worn.
A lower CPW means better value for money.
Target: under ₹150/wear for regular items.
Wardrobe Utilisation Index (WUI)
WUI = Total Wears ÷ Total Items.
Higher WUI = more use from what you own.
Target: 5+ wears per item on average.
